Photo Journal Entry: Wonders of Visayas


Visayas, one of the three main island groups in the Philippines, is located in between Luzon and Mindanao. It has plenty of cultural sites, and beautiful environments.


Nature:

Boracay Beach

This long stretch of white sand and blue water is etched onto my mind. From the soft sand, to a beautiful view, it's no wonder it's such a popular destination.

Chocolate Hills

The chocolate hills, located in Bohol, are a collection of oh so many smooth brown hills, resembling chocolate, like its name.

Hinagdanan Cave

The word "Hinagdanan" means laddered in Cebuano. To enter, one must use stairs leading to a small cave filled with limestone.


Marvelous Animals:

Tarsier

The smallest primate in the world, this species is endemic to the Philippines. Small, yet wide eyed, many of them can be found in Bohol.

so adorable!

Pawikan

These sea turtles are known for their beautiful shells. They are endangered, which is why we must take extra care to preserve and care for them.

Siquijor Buttterfly Sanctuary

Located in Siquijor province, this sanctuary has beautiful and colorful butterflies.


Religion:

Basilica del Santo Nino

Located in Cebu City, this church houses the Santo Nino. It is an old religious site filled with architectural detail.

Simala Shrine

Simala Shrine, a. k. a. The Monastery of the Holy Eucharist, is a grand religious site mostly dedicated to the Blessed Virgin Mary.

beautiful gardens!

Taoist Temple

This temple with Chinese architecure, located in Cebu, is a very popular religious site. There are 81 steps, for 81 chapters of the Taoism scripture.
